About Jay

Jay Osborne is an aspiring web developer based in San Francisco. Having spent more than 6 years designing a wide variety of buildings at various Bay Area architecture firms, he is now looking for wider horizons. Jay wants to develop the skills that can provide value for innumerable people, and serve others for decades to come. With this in mind, he has joined Rithm, a highly-ranked and well-respected coding bootcamp that allows people to rapidly upgrade their skills to meet the professional needs of the most innovative companies on earth.
